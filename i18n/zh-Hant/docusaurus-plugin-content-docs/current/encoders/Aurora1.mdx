# Aurora1 AV1

[Aurora1 AV1](https://visionular.ai/aurora1-av1-encoder/) is a proprietary and paid software AV1 encoder developed by [Visionular](https://www.visionular.com/en). Although they do provide a contact form to get a free trial, not much is known about this encoder other than cherry-picked claims and proof provided by the company themselves that it is supposedly "better" than public, FOSS encoders.
